Q: Where is the restaurant located?

A: On the first floor of the SpringHill Suites at 19 Radcliffe Dr Moosic, PA 18507. Located in the beautiful Moosic Mountains of Pennsylvania.


Q: What type of cuisine do you offer?

A: We serve classic Western-American homestyle breakfast. Including hearty waffle/pancake platters and piping hot skillet dishes.


Q: What is the general theme and atmosphere of the restaurant like?

A: It is our aim to offer a distinct feeling of being high in the mountains; in a lodge where the first brave American pioneers could be found eating the most important meal of the day.


Q: Do you only offer breakfast?

A: Breakfast and Brunch.


Q: Do you offer any specials throughout the week?

A: Of course! Please refer to our calendar of events for more information on brunch specials etc.
